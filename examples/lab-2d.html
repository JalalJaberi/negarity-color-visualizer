CIE L*a*b* Color Space - a* vs b* Plane

What is this?

This visualization shows the CIE L*a*b* color space. L* represents lightness (0-100), a* represents green-red axis (-128 to 127), and b* represents blue-yellow axis (-128 to 127). This is a perceptually uniform color space.

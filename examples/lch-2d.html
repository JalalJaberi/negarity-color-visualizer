CIE L*C*h* Color Space - Chroma vs Hue (Polar)

What is this?

This visualization shows the CIE L*C*h* color space, which is the polar representation of Lab. L* represents lightness (0-100), C* represents chroma/saturation (0-150), and h* represents hue angle (0-360°).

YCbCr Color Space - Chromaticity Diagram

What is this?

This visualization shows the YCbCr color space using the xy chromaticity diagram. YCbCr colors are converted to RGB, then to XYZ, then to xy coordinates for display. The YCbCr gamut triangle (same as RGB) is shown to indicate the limited color range of YCbCr.
